About Us

This is a blog site,spreading knowledge about health facts,healthy habits,nutrition,naturopathy,exercise,natural beauty tips,yoga and face yoga. Different sections explains facts,provides tips and some important things to remember.

Goal

My goal is to create awareness about health habits.I believe that one should not use medicines and chemicals for treatment until it is really essential to do so. I am discussing aspects of food, exercises, beauty, yoga and home remedies that will help you to maintain health.Here we are going to learn simple tips to get rid of health problems. Our main aim is to use home remedies and natural things to cure problems related health.I emphasize sticking to natural and kitchen available ingredients as there are no side effects using them and are affordable to everyone.Yoga, meditation, finger poses-mudras, face yoga are wonderful ways to stay rejuvenated and young.I want to create awareness in people that health and beauty are not always achieved at cost of expensive suppliments and cosmatics, but can also be achieved by some discipline and dedication. From the start to end of the day we put efforts to become prosperous Sometimes we forget and tend to ignore the health in this competition.Lets keep in mind, that ‘A healthy mind is greatest source of treasure to find’.

Vision

Our Vision is to collect richer sources and come up with some wonderful natural things that can benefit health.India is a well-known preacher of Ayurveda and yoga. The World has started understanding the richness of yoga. Many people visit India just to get Ayurvedic and Yogic healing treatments.Here my aim is also to spread the benefits of yoga and exercise.

When my father suffered from heel pain.He took different treatments.The pain stopped, but the results were temporary.Then we studied some exercises for heel pain. Following such simple exercises such as walking on heels, worked wonders on him.From here grew my interest in finding natural and home remedies for my simple health problems. I was inspired by many videos and articles about naturopathy. You never know when a documentary, a blog post, a book might just lead someone to adopt natural ways for treatments

Here I will be writting abut Nutrition, Balanced Diet, Healthy Recipes, Simple Exercises, Face yoga, Yoga, Meditation, Mudras and Natural Remedies. You will also find amazing solutions for weight loss, skin care, hair care etc. I am going to publish articles and FAQ on weekly basis.Meanwhile, you can discuss your issues with me through emails.I will study and provide solutions for your problems along with the references. Also please, share your experiences and reviews through email.This will keep me motivated.
