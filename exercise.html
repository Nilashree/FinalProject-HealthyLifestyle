☛Exercise ☚

Physical activities are important for body fitness.Exercise keeps the body efficient to work.It improves your stamina by training your body to become more efficient and use less energy for the same amount of work. As your conditioning level improves, your heart rate and breathing rate return to resting levels much sooner from strenuous activity. Exercise is also a key to weight control because it burns calories. If you burn off more calories than you take in, you lose weight. It's as simple as that. Here we will discuss some easy and effective exercises.

⚑ Body Planks:

The plank (also called a front hold, hover, or abdominal bridge) is an isometric core strength exercise that involves maintaining a position similar to a push-up for the maximum possible time.It will help you to reduce your risk of injury in back and spinal column.It will aslo help you to boost your metabolism. You’ll significantly improve your posture and overall balance.You will also be able to witness mental fitness.

Remember ☝: You need to be cautious doing Planking exercises if you ahve risks like Prolapse After prolapse surgery Pelvic pain conditions Weak or poorly functioning pelvic floor muscles Previous childbirth Overweight applied to you

⚑ Jogging:

Jogging is one of the oldest and most popular forms of aerobic exercise.Jogging Helps Prevent Hypertension and Heart Disease.It also Prevent Some Cancers. Jogging also helps control and prevents diabetes. A sedentary lifestyle and being overweight has been shown to directly increase your risk of developing diabetes. Adopting jogging and a healthy lifestyle can prevent you from acquiring this debilitating condition. You are also able to improve your over-all demeanor since jogging helps release the feel-good hormones called endorphins. These are chemicals released into the body that causes you to have an upbeat mood. This is what is commonly referred to as the runners high.

Remember ☝: As always, consult a physician first before embarking on any exercise program. When you do take it up, start slowly. Take brisk walks at first and slowly introduce jogging spurts as you build your endurance.

⚑ Squats:

Squats are a full-body fitness staple that work the hips, glutes, quads, and hamstrings, and sneakily strengthen the core. Squats may help improve balance and coordination, as well as bone density . Squats obviously help to build your leg muscles (including your quadriceps, hamstrings, and calves), but they also create an anabolic environment, which promotes body-wide muscle building.They prevent Injuries and also helps to Boost Your Sports Performance -- Jump Higher and Run Faster.

Remember ☝:It's especially helpful for women who often skip the weight room. Don't fear the squat, just learn to do it safely.

⚑ Lunges:

Lunges are an easy and efficient exercise to help build strength in your quadriceps, glutes, hamstrings, calves, and core. They are also relatively safe to do as they have simple movements that are easy to follow, and lunges require no special equipment to complete. Lunges target most, if not all, of the muscles in your lower body. Don't be fooled into thinking that lunges only help your legs, they also require the development of your abdominal and lower back muscles to stabilize your core and provide balance

Remember ☝:Performing additional lunges on the weaker side can help these muscles to develop at a slightly faster rate, allowing them to catch up with your stronger side. This will aid in sports and physical activities, as well as lower the risk of injury because of imbalance.
