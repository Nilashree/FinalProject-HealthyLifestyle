I am an IT masters student at Towson University.This website is developed as a curriculum project under the fundamentals of web technologies course.We were supposed to choose the topic that interests us of, on which we can create a blog site as this. Under this course, we learned various basic concepts of web technologies like HTML, CSS frameworks, javascript, jquery and have implemented them on this project.

The reason why I chose this subject because, in today's world of competition, stress, and challenges it's very important to stay healthy, in order have to stay on track with success and our goals.I am very much interested in naturopathy, yoga, exercise and always read and follow articles based on these topics.So I thought it would be great to share some easy and affordable tips and information about a healthy lifestyle through this website.I want to spread awareness of nutrition, yoga, natural remedies and exercise among all the visitors of the site.I have tried to share information about nutrition, face yoga, and exercise.Also, FAQ section answers health related questions asked by visitors. There is link section on the page which will give you a reference of the website and materials, where you can explore more about face yoga, nutrition, beauty tips, and exercise.

At the end, I would like to say that this website is an effort for the final project for the course.It might not have got that professional look, but it has cleared various concepts of web development and given me a confidence for future web development.I am very happy with the journey so far and plan to take this basic webdevelopment knowledge to next level.
