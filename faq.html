☛Which fruits should be consumed for weight Loss?

Fruits play a vital role in weight loss plan as they contain fibers as well as minerals.If you are participating in a weight loss challenge and aim at shedding away weight in a quick way, then I would suggest you consuming all variants of berries like blueberries,strawberry, cranberry, and raspberry.Under a weight loss challenge, you may need to follow strict diet and there are chances that it might affect your immunity.So berries are rich in vitamin C that increases your immunity.Also, blueberries are special fruit with high antioxidant levels.So they help in reduction of free radicals thus adding glow on your face.According to latest study at Texas Women’s University blueberries fight fat cells.If you are not under strict diet plan and heavy exercise routine you can also consume fruits like apples, pears, kiwis, oranges, and peaches. If you are eating bananas then beware because compared to other fruits, bananas are slightly higher in calories and carbs, so no monkey business: stick to one five-inch portion a day. This ensures you’ll reap all the fruit’s benefits—without expanding your waistline.

☛What are protein rich vegetarian sources?

Pulses like moong, moth beans, kidney beans and lentils are rich sources of protein.In greens broccoli is a source of protein. Soya chunks and Tofu also contains protein. Greek Yogurt is a very rich dairy source of protein.

☛ What is the best food for belly fat reduction?

Only exercises cannot help in reduction of belly fat. The main root cause of belly fat is less amount of fibre in the diet.Always include a plateful of salad in your deit. Cucumber boosts metabolism and can help in belly fat reduction.Cabbage ,lettuce and other salads can also be helpful.

☛ What should be ideal daily intake of protein?

Most of the official nutrition organizations recommend moderate intake of 56 grams of protein for an average sedimentary man and 46 grams for average sedimentary women. For muscle building this amount varies from 96 grams to 123 grams and high-intensity training might need to consume 135 grams of protein.
